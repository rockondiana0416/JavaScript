A little bit about me.

Building a website, to me, is like putting together a puzzle. What ever your dream is of having in your website. That is my puzzle and I want to put it together for you. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. A curiosity that opened a door that can never be shut.

I want to work with you to create the puzzle of your world. My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5, CSS and javaScript by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible. Always learning what is new and staying up to date is important to me as a web developer.

My chosen career path is in Web Development. Being curious about the internet, and learning about computers has always been of big interest to me. I started at Long Beach Community College with no real knowledge of how to design a website. I just know I want to do it and I can. I've joined coding websites, became a member and Treasurer of my College's Code Vikings coding club, and taken online courses. I've also received two scholarships so I can continue my education at LBCC. Send me an email at rockondiana0416@yahoo.com, I would love to talk about us working together.
